About

A bit about myself:
I'm the executive director of XdHacks Mini Vancouver 2025-2026 (ambassador director '24-25), and the founder of the global TEMP (tech, engineering, mathematics and physics) girls community HER Network. I'm part of our school's tech leadership team and am a math tutor outside of school. Some awards that I received includes Honor with Distinction (school), AP Scholar with Distinction, and Technovation Girls' 25 semi-finalists. I also love to do volunteering. I'm the co-founder of the Pet Pals club in my high school, which we support local animal shelters through fundraising and volunteering, and constantly volunteer at school and community events gaining over 50 hours of volunteering in school and over 100 hours outside of school. Outside of academics, I'm a huge horse girl, play badminton, ski, and love to go traveling, watch movies, take photos, and read books.
I would love to meet like-minded people, and I am actively looking for internships or other opportunities in the STEM fields, so feel free to reach out!
